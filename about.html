Our Story

From humble beginnings to becoming a leader in African agritech innovation

Pioneering Agritech in Africa

Africa Agrotech Consulting was founded in 2008 with a vision to transform traditional farming practices across Africa through the adoption of cutting-edge technology and innovative approaches.

What started as a small initiative to help local farmers in Kenya access better farming technologies has grown into a pan-African organization operating in 24 countries and impacting over 25,000 farmers.

Our journey has been guided by a commitment to sustainable development, food security, and economic empowerment through agricultural innovation.
