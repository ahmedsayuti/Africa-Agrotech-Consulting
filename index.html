About Africa Agrotech Consulting

Africa Agrotech Consulting (AAC) was founded with a vision to transform traditional farming practices across Africa through the adoption of cutting-edge technology and innovative approaches.

Our team of experts combines deep agricultural knowledge with technical expertise to deliver solutions that address the unique challenges faced by African farmers and agribusinesses.

"We believe that technology has the power to revolutionize African agriculture, creating sustainable livelihoods and ensuring food security for generations to come."

Mercy Maigut, Founder & CEO
